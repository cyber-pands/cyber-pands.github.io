Securing and Modernising Legacy Systems in the Context of Modern Cyber Threats

Final Year Dissertation ¬∑ 2022

This dissertation explores the security challenges posed by legacy systems in modern organisational environments. It examines why organisations continue to rely on legacy systems, the types of vulnerabilities commonly found in such systems, and the cyber attacks they are most exposed to. Through qualitative, interview-based analysis, the research provides insight into organisational perspectives and proposes practical strategies for securing legacy systems along with a prototype framework for their gradual and efficient upgrade.
